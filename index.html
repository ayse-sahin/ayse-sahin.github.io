Looking for a lab home in cognition research

I'm exploring the territory between cognitive psychology and the brain. My current interests sit around neuroimaging and neurotechnology — EEG, fMRI, and brain–computer interfaces — though I'm still in the early phase of figuring out which specific questions I want to spend years on.

I trained as an experimental psychologist in Türkiye and Spain, and recently joined Prof. Marianne Maertens's Computational Psychology Lab at TU Berlin, where I'm getting hands-on exposure to psychophysics and computational modeling of visual perception.
